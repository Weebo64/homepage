About Me

Hey there! I'm Weebo (also known as Weebo64), a 23-year-old hobby developer with a passion for bringing creative ideas to life. I specialize in creating custom 3D models for Super Mario 64 and Mario Kart Wii, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in these classic games.

When I'm not modeling, I'm actively contributing to the modding community as a code and community contributor for Retro Rewind, leader and developer for Silly Kart Wii, developer for Insane Kart Wii, and moderator for RetroWFC and IKWFC. I love being part of communities that keep these games alive and thriving.
