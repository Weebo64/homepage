About

WeebOwO is a versatile Discord bot created by Weebo64! She's a cute cat-themed bot with 98 commands that bring life and fun to your server :3c

WeebOwO offers a wide variety of features: From fun commands like /weebowo for cute images, /pat, /hug, and /ship for social interactions, to music features with YouTube & Spotify support. She also has moderation tools (AutoMod, warnings, logging), server management (starboard, suggestions, birthdays, streaming notifications), utility commands (AFK system, reminders, user info), and even special features like Mario Kart Wii friend codes, image manipulation (caption, troytify), and trivia & Akinator games!

With her friendly UwU style and many easter eggs, WeebOwO makes every server a cozier place 💕
