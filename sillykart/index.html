ABOUT THE PROJECT

🎪 THE VISION

Silly Kart Wii is my first Mario Kart Wii modpack, designed to bring fun, chaos, and creativity to the game. Built on the solid foundation of Pulladium (a community-maintained fork of Pulsar), this pack combines established features with custom silly modifications to create a unique racing experience.

The goal is simple: make Mario Kart Wii even more fun with crazy physics, silly modes, and quality-of-life improvements that enhance the experience for both casual players and competitive racers.

🛠️ BUILT WITH

Pulladium Engine Community-maintained Pulsar fork
Kamek Code injection framework
IKWFC Online connectivity service
